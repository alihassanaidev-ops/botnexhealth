# ScaleNexus — Product Overview

*A plain-language guide to what our AI voice platform does for your clinic today,
and what we're building next.*

> This document is written for clinic owners, practice managers, and their teams —
> not engineers. It describes the product in everyday terms: what it does, what it
> means for your front desk, and what your patients experience. There is no
> technical jargon here.

---

## In one sentence

ScaleNexus is an AI-powered communication platform for dental and healthcare
clinics. Today it **answers your phones**, books appointments, and keeps your
front desk in the loop — around the clock. Next, it will **reach out to your
patients for you**, automatically confirming visits, sending reminders,
re-engaging patients you haven't seen in a while, and following up with new leads.

---

# Part 1 — What the product does today

Today's product is your **always-on front desk assistant**. When a patient calls,
an AI voice agent answers, holds a natural conversation, and gets things done —
booking, rescheduling, answering questions — then hands your staff a clean record
of every call. Nothing is missed, even after hours.

## Answering and handling calls

Your clinic's phone line is answered by an AI voice agent that can:

- **Pick up every call, 24/7** — no busy signals, no voicemail black holes, no
  missed opportunities when your team is with patients or the office is closed.
- **Hold a natural conversation** — patients speak normally; the agent
  understands and responds like a helpful receptionist.
- **Verify who's calling** — before sharing any personal information, the agent
  confirms the caller's identity using their date of birth plus a second detail
  (email or the last four digits of their phone number).
- **Transfer to a real person when needed** — if a call needs a human (for
  example, a billing question or an emergency), the agent connects the patient to
  the right department.

## Booking and managing appointments

Over the phone, the agent can handle the full appointment lifecycle:

- **Book new appointments** — the patient chooses a visit type and preferred
  provider, and the agent offers real available time slots.
- **Reschedule or cancel** existing appointments in real time.
- **Add brand-new patients** — if a caller isn't in your system yet, the agent can
  create their profile on the spot so they can book right away.
- **Respect your schedule** — the times offered always honor your operating hours,
  lunch breaks, and buffer times, so patients are never offered a slot that doesn't
  actually work for you.

Every booking is written **directly into your existing patient-management
system**, so your records stay accurate and up to date without any manual entry.

## Works with your existing systems

- The platform **connects to the practice-management software you already use**
  (such as Dentrix, Eaglesoft, or Open Dental) through a single, secure
  integration.
- **You don't have to change anything or integrate it yourself** — we handle all
  of the setup behind the scenes.
- Availability, providers, appointment types, and patient records all stay in sync
  with your existing system in real time.

## Your staff dashboard

Clinic staff get a simple web dashboard that gives full visibility into every
call:

- **Complete call records** — a written transcript of every conversation, plus a
  short AI-written summary of what happened.
- **Call organization** — tag and sort calls by outcome (booked, inquiry, needs
  follow-up, and so on).
- **A callback queue** — any call the agent couldn't fully resolve is queued so
  your team can follow up.
- **Daily metrics** — see call volume, appointments booked, and missed calls at a
  glance.
- **Notifications** — get alerted about important calls by email, text message, or
  right inside the dashboard.
- **Custom messaging** — manage your own email templates and wording.

## Built for clinics of every size

- **Single practices** run the agent on their line with their own hours and
  settings.
- **Multi-location practices** run it independently at each office — each location
  has its own phone number, hours, and connection to your records.
- **Clinic groups (DSOs)** can add an oversight account to monitor activity across
  all of their practices, while each practice's patient data stays completely
  separate and private.

Staff access is controlled by **role**, so people only see what they should — a
front-desk team member sees their own location's calls and patients, while an
administrator can manage settings across the whole practice.

## Privacy and security you can trust

Healthcare communication carries real responsibility, and the platform is built
for it:

- **HIPAA-ready** by design.
- **Patient information is encrypted** and protected at all times.
- **Every clinic's data is walled off** from every other clinic's.
- **Secure staff sign-in** with multi-factor authentication.
- **Text-message consent is respected** — patients who opt out are never
  contacted again by text.
- **A full activity log** records staff actions for compliance and peace of mind.

# Part 2 — What we're building next

Today's product is **inbound** — it answers the calls that come to you. The next
major capability we're developing turns that around: the **Outbound Engagement
Engine** lets the platform **reach out to your patients for you**, automatically
and intelligently.

The idea is simple: *every clinic should be able to keep its schedule full and its
patients cared for without staff spending hours on the phone every day.*

## From reactive to proactive

Instead of your team manually calling patients to confirm visits, chase reminders,
or win back people who've drifted away, the system will do it — across **voice
calls, text messages, and email** — on a schedule you control, in your clinic's
local time zone, and built on the same trusted AI that already handles your
inbound calls.

## The four outreach campaigns

The Outbound Engagement Engine centers on four ready-to-use campaigns:

| Campaign | What it does | Why it helps your clinic |
|---|---|---|
| **Appointment Confirmation** | Reaches out before a scheduled visit to confirm the patient is coming | Fewer no-shows; you can plan each day with confidence |
| **Appointment Reminders** | Sends timely reminders the day before or day of a visit | Patients show up, and show up on time |
| **Overdue Patient Recall** | Finds patients who haven't been in for a while and re-engages them | Fills your schedule with returning patients who had drifted away |
| **Sales Qualification** | Engages new inbound leads, gauges their interest, and books the good ones | Converts new-patient interest into booked visits without manual chasing |

## Reaching patients the right way

- **Three channels** — voice calls, text messages, and email.
- **Your choice of order** — for example, "try a call first, then a text, then an
  email if there's no response."
- **Always checked against your live schedule** — before any reminder or
  confirmation goes out, the system verifies the appointment still exists, so
  patients are never contacted about a visit that was already cancelled or moved.

## Full control for your team

The engine is designed to be **configured, not coded** — built for clinic staff,
not engineers. Your team will be able to:

- Turn each campaign **on or off per location**.
- Set the **timing** (for example, "first attempt 72 hours before the
  appointment").
- Choose **which channels** each campaign uses and **customize the wording**.
- Define **quiet hours** so patients are never contacted too early or too late.
- Set **retry rules** — how many times to try, and when to stop.
- Watch everything happen on a **real-time progress dashboard**.

## Smart and considerate by default

- **Quiet-hours enforcement** — the system won't call or text outside the hours
  you set, in each patient's local time zone.
- **Automatic opt-out handling** — if a patient replies "STOP" or opts out, they
  are immediately removed from further contact.
- **No over-contacting** — suppression and do-not-contact rules make sure patients
  aren't pestered across campaigns or locations.
- **Smart retries and fallbacks** — if a call goes unanswered, the system can move
  to the next channel, and it stops as soon as the goal is met (for example, once
  a patient confirms).

## Richer patient insight

As part of this work, we're **deepening the connection to your practice-management
system** so campaigns can be smarter and safer — drawing on visit history,
recalls, insurance and billing context, and treatment-plan information where it's
relevant. Sensitive information is always handled on a **minimum-necessary**
basis and protected by strict access controls.

## Connecting to more practice-management systems — GoTracker

We're also expanding the range of practice-management systems the platform can
work with. A new integration with **GoTracker PMS** has been developed and is
now in its **final testing phase**.

Until now, the platform could not connect to GoTracker. This new capability
closes that gap, so clinics running GoTracker can use ScaleNexus just like clinics
on the systems we already support — with appointments, availability, and patient
records kept in sync automatically.

- **What it means for you** — if your practice uses GoTracker, you'll soon be able
  to run our AI voice agent and outreach campaigns on top of your existing records,
  with no manual data entry.
- **How it works, in plain terms** — a small, secure companion application installs
  alongside GoTracker and keeps information flowing safely between your practice
  system and our platform, so both stay up to date.
- **Built to the same standards** — the same security, privacy, and reliability
  expectations that apply everywhere else in the platform apply here too.

This is a significant step: it lets us support practice systems that previously
couldn't connect to ScaleNexus, opening the platform to more clinics.

## Reporting that ties outreach to results

The dashboard will show you what your outreach is achieving:

- **Campaign results** — confirmations delivered, reminders sent, recalls that
  turned into bookings, and leads that qualified and booked.
- **Channel performance** — which method (call, text, or email) works best for
  your patients.
- **Live progress** — a real-time view of where each patient is in a sequence.
- **Revenue attribution** — connecting booked and confirmed appointments back to
  the campaigns that drove them.

## Compliance built in from day one

Outbound communication is more heavily regulated than answering calls, so
compliance is engineered into every part of the engine:

- **HIPAA and PHIPA** protection for all patient information.
- **TCPA and CASL** adherence for calls, texts, and emails — including consent
  capture, opt-outs, quiet hours, and proper sender identification.
- **Text-messaging registration** (10DLC / A2P) handled for you where it applies.
- **Full audit trails** and **per-clinic data isolation** throughout.

Every component is being built to **production standards from day one** — there is
no throwaway demo or pilot version. As each piece is delivered, it is ready for
live clinic use.
